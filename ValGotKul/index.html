OUR STORY

Whatever our souls are made of, Nakul and Vallari’s are the same.

Living on different coasts of the country, totally unaware of what was to happen next, Nakul and Vallari were introduced to each other by a friend. And the next thing you know, numbers were exchanged, and messages were sent. And then in a few days, they spoke to each other for the first time and that was all that they needed.

That one phone call was their destiny call. When they hung up that call after hours of chit chat, they both knew that “This was it”. They had found each other without even looking. They had the keys to each other’s hearts and felt safe to open the locks and unleash their truest selves and be completely and honestly who they are. They unveil the best in each other and make life come to life.
